About me

Hello, my name is Akshay Vs and I am a 17-year-old self-taught developer. My love for computer science and programming began when I was just 14 years old, and since then, I have dedicated my free time to computer science at school :)

I have worked in a variety of different domains, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, 3D designing, game design, IoT, robotics, digital electronics, and computer hardware. I am particularly proud of the Turing complete CPU architecture that I designed called AVA (Advanced Versatile Architecture), which includes a custom assembly and operating system. Currently, I am working on my second game using Unity.

My interests are not limited to just programming and hardware design. I am also passionate about cinematography, entrepreneurship, and game development. I enjoy sharing my knowledge and experience with others through my Medium page, where I write about software development and technology on a monthly basis. Furthermore, I have contributed to multiple open-source projects.
